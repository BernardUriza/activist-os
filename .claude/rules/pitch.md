# Pitch — how to describe Activist OS externally

Governs the language used to describe the product to the outside world —
demo script, video, slides, README intro, Discord posts, submission form.
It does NOT govern how the product behaves (`safety.md`) or what the agents
do (`../../agents/*/SPEC.md`).

## TL;DR — the lines

**One-liner:**
> **"A multi-agent workflow for safe, evidence-backed civic advocacy."**

**The three lines (architecture pitch):**
> **Band coordinates the agents.**
> **FI preserves memory and provenance.**
> **Safety gates every public action.**

**Lineage line:**
> **"Insult AI cross-examined claims. Activist OS coordinates evidence-backed action."**

**Submission long-description:**
> "Activist OS is a regulated, high-stakes multi-agent workflow for ethical
> community advocacy. It coordinates specialized agents through Band for
> evidence gathering, claim verification, campaign planning, outreach
> drafting, volunteer coordination, and safety/legal review — turning
> verified evidence into nonviolent, compliant, actionable campaign packets
> while preventing doxxing, harassment, unsafe escalation, and unsupported
> claims."

## Framing order (non-negotiable)

1. FIRST: **"safe civic advocacy workflows"** — sounds enterprise/high-stakes.
2. THEN: the initial use case — vegan and ecological community campaigns,
   greenwashing detection, local action planning.

Never open with "vegan activist app" — it reads niche/ideological before it
reads serious. The cause is the use case, not the category.

## Forbidden vocabulary

| ❌ Never say | ✅ Say instead |
|---|---|
| "decentralized" / "censorship-proof" | "privacy-conscious, audit-friendly, exportable workflows" |
| "legal immunity" / "legally safe" | "legal-risk-aware by design" |
| "the AI runs your campaign" | "humans execute; the system assembles and governs" |
| "replaces organizers" | "the missing ops layer for groups with no staff" |

No system grants legal immunity. Claiming it is both false and disqualifying
in a regulated-workflows track.

## Tone

Serious, evidence-forward, constructive. The same voice in the README, the
video and Discord. No corporate fluff, no excess emoji, no activist outrage
theater — the receipts carry the emotion.
