# Video script — Activist OS demo (≤4 min)

Picture-locked voiceover plan for the submission video. Record the silent screen
capture first, narrate per phrase later (see the picture-locked workflow). Total
budget: **4:00**. The money shot is the **Safety veto → revision → approval**
loop; everything else frames it.

Live surface to record: <https://proud-stone-023fae70f.7.azurestaticapps.net/app>

---

## 0:00 – 0:20 · What Activist OS is

> Activist OS turns a community concern into a safe, evidence-backed campaign
> packet. Six specialized agents coordinate through Band — and Safety can veto
> anything before it goes public.

Show: the landing (`/`). The one-liner and the agent chain diagram.

## 0:20 – 0:45 · Open the console

> This is the live console. The chat surface is fi-glass; everything you see is
> rendered from the real transport, not narrated.

Show: navigate to `/app`. The fi-glass `AgentConversationSurface`, the
`TRANSPORT` badge, `MODE LIVE`.

## 0:45 – 1:20 · Submit a civic concern

> I submit one concern: a restaurant's "compostable" claim with no local disposal
> evidence. One input — and the coordination begins.

Show: type the concern, hit Enter. The first handoffs start streaming in.

## 1:20 – 1:55 · Band coordination, visible

> Band coordinates the agents in a real room. The provenance chip shows the room
> id. Eight handoffs, six Band messages, two virtual events for the reporter and
> system legs.

Show: the room provenance chip, `TRANSPORT BAND`, the 8-handoff sequence
(evidence → campaign → safety → campaign → safety → outreach → coordinator →
reporter), the message/event counts.

## 1:55 – 2:40 · The money shot — Safety veto loop

> Here is the point. Campaign drafts: *"Restaurant X is lying to its customers."*
> Safety **vetoes** it — defamation, an unsupported accusation against a named
> target. Campaign revises. Safety **approves** the evidence-bounded version. The
> rejection stays on the record.

Show: handoff index 2 — the `SAFETY_VETO` verdict with `blocked_items` and the
reviewer note. Then the revision. Then handoff index 4 — `SAFETY_APPROVED`.
**Linger on the veto card — this is the differentiator.**

## 2:40 – 3:20 · Artifacts rail

> The packet assembles on the right: the evidence brief with provenance tiers,
> the safety gate including the rejected revision, and the final campaign packet.

Show: open Evidence Brief, Safety Gate (scroll to the rejected revision),
Campaign Packet in the artifacts rail.

## 3:20 – 3:45 · Human-in-the-loop

> Notice what never happened: nothing was published. The system assembles a safe
> packet. Humans hold the send button.

Show: the packet ready state — no "publish" action, only human task hand-off.

## 3:45 – 4:00 · Close

> Band coordinates the agents. FI preserves provenance. Safety gates every public
> action. That's Activist OS.

Show: back to `/app` overview or the landing, end card.
