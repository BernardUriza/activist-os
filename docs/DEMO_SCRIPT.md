# Demo Script (target: ≤ 4 minutes)

## Cold open (0:00–0:30)

> "A local restaurant chain labels its packaging '100% compostable'. It isn't. A community group wants to act — but a wrong public accusation can get them sued, and a careless post can dox an employee. Activism is a high-stakes workflow with no ops layer. This is Activist OS."

## Act 1 — The concern goes in (0:30–1:00)

Type the concern into the UI. Show the system declaring its plan before acting (plan-before-act transparency). Point at the agent roster: six agents, coordinated through Band.

## Act 2 — Evidence with receipts (1:00–1:45)

Show the `evidence_brief`: claims marked supported / partial / unsupported, each source tagged by provenance tier (fetched fulltext vs news search vs company source). One claim comes back **unsupported** — highlight that it gets blocked from use, not buried.

## Act 3 — The safety gate earns its keep (1:45–2:45) ← the money shot

CAMPAIGN drafts a plan that includes a risky line (names a store manager). SAFETY **vetoes** it through Band — show the structured verdict: `needs_revision`, check `doxxing: fail`, note attached. CAMPAIGN revises, resubmits, gets `approved`. Show the audit log entry of the whole exchange.

> "The veto you just saw is the product. Every public action passes a gate, and every gate decision is on the record."

## Act 4 — From approval to action (2:45–3:30)

Show the outputs fan out: OUTREACH produces emails/posts/flyer copy, COORDINATOR produces the volunteer task board. REPORTER assembles the campaign packet — flip through it: narrative, copy, tasks, provenance report, safety audit log.

## Close (3:30–4:00)

> "Band coordinates the agents. FI preserves memory and provenance. Safety gates every public action. Insult AI cross-examined claims — Activist OS coordinates evidence-backed action. Built on Band, for the people doing the work."

## Demo checklist

- [ ] Seed concern reproducible offline (cache the evidence fetches — live scraping mid-demo is russian roulette)
- [ ] The veto moment scripted and rehearsed (it is the differentiator)
- [ ] Band coordination visible on screen (message exchange / handoff view), not just narrated
- [ ] Total runtime under 4 minutes; first 30 seconds carry the stakes
